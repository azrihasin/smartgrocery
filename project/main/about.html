         The purpose of this webpage is to educate people about grocery items or cooking ingredient especially the item that rare or hard to recognize. The aim of this webpage also is to make shopping easier for the people that don't usually go to the supermarket. This can be very useful for city folks who is very busy with their works. Sometimes people who already familiar with groceries item also can be confuse and mistaken. So, in this web page, all grocery item will be inserted with pictures and detailed facts, so users can recognize the item easily. Every item will be included with nutritional content for the users to manage their nutrition intake. In addition, this web page will display cooking recipe that related with the item in every item section. Therefore, we hope this web page can reduce the hassle to buy groceries among all people
